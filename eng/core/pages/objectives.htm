Mision
The mission of Colombian Airways is to base and share the professionalism of an air simulation carrying out national and international operations as a virtual airline created and founded in Colombia carrying the name of our country high in the IVAO network.


Vision
To be the leading airline in Colombia and recognized by its professionalism and commitment in the network as well as to be the virtual airline with the best recognition for its quality, warmth and professionalism with its pilots and operation.
